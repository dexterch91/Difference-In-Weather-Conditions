# Difference-In-Weather-Conditions
Temperature plays a significant component to the experience of comfort in a space. With the new Housing Development Board (HDB) Tengah town scheduled to complete its first build in 2023, residents will be moving into the vicinity to their new homes. This article guides a stepwise walkthrough analysis of the weather differences between regions of interests (Jurong West, Tengah and Changi) within Singapore and provide expectations on the weather conditions that new homeowners will most likely experience should they originally live in the extreme West or East side of Singapore.
